# Chorely Brand Guide

## Brand Position

Chorely helps families turn everyday chores into earned allowance and growing responsibility. The identity should feel warm, homey, and trustworthy — like something made for real families, not a corporate dashboard. Parents should feel it belongs on the fridge as much as on a phone.

The logo mark is a welcoming house with a golden heart inside: home, care, and reward in one shape. It is flat, friendly, and professional — never childish or sticker-like, and never cold or corporate.

## Typography

Recommended type families:

- Inter
- Manrope
- Sora

The wordmark is `Chorely` in title case with bold weight and tight, friendly letter spacing. Use sentence case for supporting product copy. Avoid handwritten novelty fonts and avoid all-caps body copy.

## Logo Usage

Clear space should be at least one-half of the icon width around the full logo. For compact UI headers, maintain at least 16 px clear space around the mark.

Minimum sizes:

- Full horizontal logo: 240 px wide.
- Website header logo: 144 px wide.
- App icon: 48 px square in UI contexts.
- Favicon: 16 px square minimum.

## Do And Do Not

Do:

- Keep the mark flat, crisp, and vector-sharp.
- Preserve the proportions between icon and wordmark.
- Use gold for the heart and small reward accents.
- Maintain strong contrast on light and dark surfaces.

Do not:

- Add cartoon characters or chore-chart stickers to the logo itself.
- Stretch, skew, rotate, or recolor the logo outside the approved palette.
- Add heavy gradients, shadows, bevels, or glossy effects.
- Place the full tagline version at favicon or tiny app-icon sizes.
